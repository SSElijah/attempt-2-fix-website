Bidoof

Based off of tons of research, this result may shock you...

What the heck is even that?

Alright, hear me out. This companion will change your life for the better. Have you ever been attacked by intruders? Have you ever felt threatened? Fear no more, your savior is here. This companions's name is Bidoof, which kind of resembles a rodent due to its dark brown snout, red nose, and bushy cheeks. It also has 2 large teeth/incisors on its upper jaw used for gnawing wood and rocks. Why get a chainsaw when you can get a Bidoof? Unlike dogs, this companion can be found and tamed in the wild, near small bodies of water, around lakes or rivers. They tend to build dams, so as long as you wait outside their natural habitat, you have a chance to recruit your own Bidoof!

Bibarel

Now here's what's crazy, unlike other companions, this little guy evolves. Say Bidoof is a level 1 crook, this little fella is now a level 50 boss. Once you feed your companion enough, it'll become a Bibarel. This upgraded companion can not only cut down trees but also build structures. So when you grow old and can't build a fire, Bibarel will assist you by bringing all the wood in a pile. Overall, Bidoof would make a great companion for those who live in the woods due to its expertiese in the forest environment. Who wouldn't want a Bidoof/Bibarel as a companion?
