The Dog

Based off of tons of research, I have come to the conclusion a dog is one of the top 3 companions to own.

What do dogs do?

Dogs are animals that thrive off of companionship. They enjoy running, playing, exploring, and meeting other dogs or people. Dogs are can protect their owner depending on the breed and how they are trained. In general though, dogs bark a lot so they are kind of like a walking security system. They also have a strong sense of smell so when you're trying to watch the game eating a sandwich, you'll have a free footrest depending on where your dog sits and when they give up trying to get a bite of your sandwich. Overall, dogs are such wonderful creatures that will always being happiness to your life and the world in general.

What is a cool dog?

The Shiba Inu is what most people consider "a cool dog", I mean, who wouldnt like the dog that was the face of several crypto coins? Also theres some cool dog lore behind these amazing dogs. Based off of ancient history, these dogs where bred as hunters in Japan over 60 years ago and made their way to Western countries.
